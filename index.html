About

Jason has over 15 years of experience in preparing sushi. He has been preparing sushi for customers all over the tri-state area and has catered for countless parties, concerts, and events.

With dozens of different kinds of sushi, onigiri, and appetizers, we will surely have something for everyone.

Hosting a party? No worries! Jason does party trays of all sizes. Just tell us what you want and we'll be sure to accomadate you in the best way possible.

Craving something that's not on the menu, or need to customize a roll? Let us know, and Jason will be able to prepare it for you.
